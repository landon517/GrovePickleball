About Us

We are Grove Pickleball, a new pickleball community based in Dallas, TX, with efforts to expand throughout the Texas Triangle. Our mission is to promote the sport of pickleball to players of all levels and to bring sensible awareness to environmental issues with donations and charity work. We hope you will consider joining our community!
